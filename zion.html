App Goal

The purpose of this app is to provide park visitors and soon to be visitors a place to learn how to navigate the park and get to different trailheads throughout the park via the shuttle surface. The “trails” page can be found at the bottom navigation and gives users the ability to filter trails from easy, moderate and strenuous, depending on what kind of hike they are wanting to do gives users the ability to filter trails from easy, moderate and strenuous, depending on what kind of hike they are wanting to do.

Saved Trails & Shuttle Links

Each trail “card” allows users to view a link to the shuttle stop for that trail, saved it to their “favorite hikes” on their profile, and expand the card for further information. The trail maps pages will be the main feature of the app and the driving force of why it was created in the first place.
