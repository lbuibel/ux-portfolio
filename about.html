A little bit about me

My name is Logan Uibel and I'm passionate about not just making things work, but making them work well. While studying web and app design & development at UVU, I've been able to better understand the process of building something from scratch, not just on the design side, but on the development and project management side of things as well.

Two Wheel Hobbies

When I'm not working on new projects, I like to spend my free time on two wheels. I've enjoyed cycling, both road and mountain for several years now and have just recently picked up riding motorcycles.


I truly believe the best way to experience a new place is on a bike. There's just something about being out on a bike that's hits differently than driving when it comes to experiencing a place for the first time.

Flume trail overlooking Lake Tahoe

After years of pedaling bikes, I wanted to be able to venture further out and see new places which is where motorcycles came into play. In the past year I've seen more of my home state on my motorcycle than I have in the 20+ years leading up to owning it.

Near Echo Resevoir Utah

Besides using motorcycles and bicycles as a tool to see new places, I also enjoy working on them. There's something super satisfying about taking something apart, seeing how it works, fixing it and putting it back together. I think this is why I enjoy developing and designing websites and applications so much, there's something almost mechanical about understand the pieces of a project and how they should all fit together to work properly.
